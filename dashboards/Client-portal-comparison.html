Client portal platforms: coaching-native + general services

A combined view: coaching-first platforms (built around sessions, accountability, and progress) plus general client-portal platforms (built around service delivery: onboarding, files, billing, approvals). Pricing is intentionally shown as ballpark.

Coaching-native platforms

Best when your “product” is transformation + follow-through between sessions.

Platform Best fit Ideal stage Starting price Coach-side management Client portal experience Strengths Watch-outs
CoachAccountable Accountability + continuity Solo → small team $20/mo (small client count) Plans, actions, progress, reminders, coaching record Dedicated client portal with actions + progress Most “coaching-native” follow-through Not a full marketing suite; branding is functional
Simply.Coach Structured 1:1 coaching delivery Solo → multi-coach $9/mo (starter tier) Client mgmt, forms/contracts, goals/action plans (plan dependent) Client dashboards/workspaces Clean delivery posture Plan feature limits matter
Practice Better Wellness / practitioner coaching Solo → practice $25/mo (starter, annual) Scheduling, intake, notes, progress, secure messaging Robust portal for booking/forms/messages/docs Great for forms + secure comms May feel heavy outside wellness
Kajabi Coaching + content programs Solo → scale $179/mo (basic) Offers, payments, subscriptions, content delivery Member login for program access Monetization + content delivery Less coaching-CRM behavior tracking
SuiteDash White-label “services firm” portal Small team → agency $19/mo (entry tier) CRM, tasks, files, invoicing, e-sign, scheduling White-labeled portal + branded login Premium services vibe Feature-dense; needs discipline
CoachVantage All-in-one coaching ops + portal Solo → small team $49/mo (common plan) Workflow mgmt, scheduling, resources, goals Client portal for booking + resources Practical all-in-one Verify plan limits / UX expectations
Delenta Brand-forward coaching + scale Solo → multi-coach ~$9–$29+/mo CRM, scheduling, payments, programs; team features Branded portals (incl sponsor/coaches depending on plan) Brand + scale posture Plan details/limits vary
upcoach Suite: coaching + courses + engagement Solo → scale $99/mo (annual) CRM, payments, courses, coaching activities, messaging Clients access programs/activities in one place Balanced all-in-one Some features skew cohort/community (optional)
Passion.io Mobile-first “branded app” coaching Brand-driven → scale $99/mo (annual) App delivery + engagement + monetization Branded iOS/Android + web experience Best “own app” story Not a classic CRM; fees matter

General client-portal platforms (less coaching-centric)

Best when your business is “deliver a service project” (onboarding → files → approvals → billing).

Platform Best fit Ideal stage Starting price Business-side management Client portal experience Strengths Watch-outs
ClientManager.io Freelancers + agencies wanting “simple all-in-one” Solo → small team $59/mo (monthly list) Client mgmt + proposals/contracts + invoicing Client portal for collaboration/onboarding Simple, portal + docs + billing in one place Confirm reporting depth + integrations for scale
HoneyBook Service businesses managing projects end-to-end Solo → small team $19/mo (starter) Pipeline + contracts + invoices + scheduling + automation Branded client portal per project (files/messages/details) Strong client experience for service delivery Not coaching-native; less about behavioral follow-through
Dubsado Service providers needing polished workflows Solo → small team Client portals supported (pricing varies) Workflows, forms, proposals/contracts, invoicing, scheduling Client portals for forms/docs, appointments, invoices Polished portal + automation vibe More project/service delivery than coaching tracking
Copilot (copilot.app) Pro services needing a flexible, secure portal layer Small team → scale Varies Workflow components depending on modules “Most flexible” secure client portal platform Premium portal-first positioning May require more configuration
Assembly CRM + client portal for professional services Solo → small team Varies Client mgmt, files, payments Modern portal to connect, pay, and share files Portal-first simplicity Validate depth for your service model
Workshop angle (general audience): If your workshop includes agencies/freelancers, demoing a “service delivery portal” (HoneyBook/Dubsado/ClientManager.io) can land faster than a coaching-native platform.
Workshop angle (coaching audience): Coaching-native tools win when you need repeatable accountability + continuity between sessions.
