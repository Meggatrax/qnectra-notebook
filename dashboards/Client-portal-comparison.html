Coaching platforms with client portals (for coaches + clients)

Platforms coaches commonly use to manage clients and give clients a login/portal experience. Pricing below is public list pricing (ballpark) and may vary by billing cycle, limits, or promos.

Workshop pilot recommendations (pick your demo angle)

1) Executive / Leadership coaching (Elevate Advisory)

  • CoachAccountable — best for accountability + continuity.
  • Simply.Coach — structured 1:1 delivery + “client workspace” framing.
  • SuiteDash — premium “advisory firm” portal vibe (white-label).

2) Wellness / practitioner-style coaching

  • Practice Better — forms + scheduling + secure client comms are central.

3) Program / course-led coaching

  • Kajabi — strong all-in-one monetization + member experience. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
  • Thinkific — clean learning portal; coaching layers on top. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

4) Mobile-first / high engagement coaching

  • Passion.io — branded app story lands fast in workshops. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
