Warm Lead → Client Conversion System (Coaching Niche)

Sales Navigator is your signal engine. This sequence converts signals into conversations, conversations into diagnostics, and diagnostics into paid work—without mass outreach.
Signal → Trust → Diagnosis → Paid Execution

Nurture Sequence

Run on warm leads only
A

Signal → Context

5–7 min / lead

Turn alerts + profile cues into a one-line “why now” hypothesis.

Capture 3 notes: offer type, delivery model, ops complexity (portal/onboarding/tools).
Assign list: Coaching – Warm / Coaching – Watch.
Exit: You can state the likely time leak in one sentence.
▼ micro-touches
B

Relationship → Micro-touch

2 touches / 5 days

Show attention before asking for anything. Keep it human and specific.

Comment with an operator insight (1–2 sentences).
Reply to a post; reference a real constraint (delivery, retention, ops load).
Exit: They connect, reply, or engage back.
▼ open a thread
C

Warm DM (No Pitch)

1 message

Trigger + operator observation + permission question. Create a “yes/no” reply path.

Lead with a trigger: cohort launch, new hire, frequent posting, scale language.
Ask 1 ops question: client portal, onboarding flow, delivery consistency.
Exit: They answer (even briefly).
▼ diagnose
D

3-Question Diagnostic

DM or 15–20 min

Surface the real drag. Don’t sell—map constraints.

Where’s the most drag: lead→client, onboarding, delivery, renewals?
What breaks when volume spikes?
What systems are in play (GHL/HubSpot/Kajabi/Stripe/etc.) and where do handoffs happen?
▼ convert
E

Mini-Diagnosis Offer

15-min “Ops Map”

Earn the right to propose paid work by delivering a useful artifact fast.

Deliver: quick ops map + 2 quick wins (portal/onboarding/automation).
Exit: They ask “what would it take to fix this?”
▼ paid entry
F

Paid Diagnostic Sprint (Q-Consult™)

2 weeks

Package clarity into a plan: workflow map, portal blueprint, KPI baseline, quick wins.

Outcome: prioritized roadmap + implementation sequence.
Bridge: “If we build this, do you want it done with you or for you?”
▼ execution
G

Execution Retainer (Q-Engaged™)

Build + integrate

Client portal, CRM cleanup, automations, reporting, governance—so scale stays clean.

Build: portal + onboarding + delivery workflows.
Measure: cycle time + drop-off + manual hours reclaimed.
Operator rule: Don’t pitch before warm. Warm means you’ve earned a response—then you diagnose.
