Use this to validate your coaching ICP on LinkedIn and ensure your Sales Nav subscription compounds. Select a score using the colored selectors. Notes persist locally in your browser.

Checkpoints

Key question at 30 days
Do I clearly recognize who belongs — and who doesn’t?
Profiles reviewed 100–150
Qualified leads saved 30–50
False positives trend Decreasing week-over-week
Distinct patterns identified ≥5
ICP confidence Medium+ (can describe a real buyer)
Operator note: At 30 days, clarity matters more than outreach. If it still feels random, refine lists + filters.
Key question at 60 days
Can I segment this market into subtypes I understand?
Profiles reviewed (cumulative) 250–300
Qualified leads saved (cumulative) 60–80
Distinct ICP subtypes 3–4
Strong-signal lists ≥2 lists produce good leads reliably
Messaging readiness High (you know what to lead with)
Operator note: At 60 days, you should be able to say “we serve these subtypes best” and exclude the rest.
Key question at 90 days
Is this a niche I can consistently reach, qualify, and engage on LinkedIn?
Profiles reviewed (cumulative) 400–500
Clean lead bench 100–150 outreach-ready leads
Outreach confidence High (who + why is obvious)
Credit efficiency Low waste; credits tied to objectives
Channel clarity LinkedIn is viable primary channel
Operator note: At 90 days, you should know exactly who to message and why. If not, pause or exit the channel.
